ABOUT THE 32-BIT SAGE

Greetings, fellow retro enthusiast! I'm the 32-Bit Sage, your guide through the golden age of gaming.

Since getting my first NES in 1989, I've been obsessed with preserving gaming history. This site is my love letter to the 16/32-bit era that shaped my childhood.

When I'm not writing about old games, you can find me speedrunning Super Metroid or collecting rare Japanese imports.
